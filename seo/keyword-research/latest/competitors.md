# Competitor Analysis - 2026-01-25

## Top Competitors Discovered
1. **Asian Spine Hospital** (`asianspinehospital.com`)
   - **Strategy**: Focuses heavily on "Full Endoscopic Spine Surgery".
   - **Key Pages**: Specific procedure pages with "Advantages" sections.
   - **Weakness**: Very clinical, less patient-centric storytelling.

2. **Yashoda Hospitals** (`yashodahospitals.com`)
   - **Strategy**: "Best Doctors" lists and "Robotic Surgery" tech focus.
   - **Key Pages**: High domain authority "Best Spine Surgeons" listicles.
   - **Gap**: Broad targeting, less specific on niche conditions like "Lumbar Spondylosis exercises".

3. **Dr. Raveesh Sunkara** (`drraveesh.com`)
   - **Strategy**: Personal branding + Cost transparency ("Cost of Endoscopic Spine Surgery").
   - **Key Signal**: Price transparency is a click magnet.

4. **Apollo Hospitals** (`apollohospitals.com`)
   - **Strategy**: Big brand trust, directory style.

## Insights
- **"Cost" keywords** are high intent. We have `spine-surgery-cost-hyderabad` but can reinforce it.
- **"Exercises" and "Non-surgical"** are top TOFU/MOFU entry points.
- **"Lumbar Spondylosis"** is a specific condition often searched by older adults who want to avoid surgery.
