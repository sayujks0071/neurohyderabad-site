# Competitor Gap Analysis: Spine Surgery Hyderabad

## Top Competitors Analyzed
1. **Dr. Raveesh Sunkara (drraveesh.com)**
   - **Strengths:** Explicit cost tables, detailed recovery timelines, high-volume blog strategy targeting long-tail keywords (e.g., "life after spinal fusion 5 years").
   - **Schema:** Uses `MedicalWebPage`, `FAQPage`.
   - **UX:** Clear "Call Now" CTAs, sticky header, table of contents.

2. **Yashoda Hospitals** (Analyzed via SERP)
   - **Strengths:** highly authoritative "Procedure Overview" cards (Duration, Anesthesia, Recovery).
   - **Trust:** Emphasizes hospital infrastructure and multi-specialty support.

## Content Gaps for Dr. Sayuj
| Feature | Competitor (Dr. Raveesh) | Dr. Sayuj (Current Status) | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cost Transparency** | Dedicated table with range (₹2L - ₹3.5L) | Mentioned in some blogs, not central | Add a "Cost Transparency" section to Service pages with ranges. |
| **Procedure Details** | Step-by-step (1. Prep, 2. Incision...) | Technical descriptions | Simplify into a "Patient Journey" 5-step visual/list. |
| **Recovery Timeline** | Specific weeks (1-3 weeks) for return to work | General advice | Create a "Recovery Roadmap" infographic/list. |
| **Success Rates** | Claims "80-90% success" | Evidence-based claims | Add a "Clinical Outcomes" section with data. |
| **Long-tail Blogs** | "Life 5 years after surgery", "Office chair back pain" | Medical/Technical focus | Add lifestyle/patient-centric blog topics. |

## Structural Gaps
- **Navigation:** Competitors have granular dropdowns for every specific condition.
- **Interlinking:** Competitors heavily interlink "Symptoms" blogs to "Treatment" service pages.
